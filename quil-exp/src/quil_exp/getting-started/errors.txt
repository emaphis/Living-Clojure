(q/ellipse-mode :corner) - ellipseMode(CORNER);
draws object in wrong location.
:center - specifies the location of the ellipse as the center of the shape (default).
:radius - similar to center, but the width and height parameters to ellipse specify the radius of the ellipse, rather than the diameter.
:corner - draws the shape from the upper-left corner of its bounding box.
:corners - uses the four parameters to ellipse to set two opposing corners of the ellipse's bounding box.
